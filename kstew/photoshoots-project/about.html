Biography

Robert Pattinson is an English actor, producer, and model. He gained worldwide fame for his role as Edward Cullen in the "Twilight" series. Since then, he has taken on a variety of roles in independent films and major blockbusters.

Career Highlights

  • Starred in the "Twilight" series (2008-2012)
  • Featured in films such as "The Lighthouse" (2019) and "Tenet" (2020)
  • Cast as Bruce Wayne in "The Batman" (2022)

Personal Life

Pattinson is known for his privacy and has often kept details of his personal life away from the public eye. He enjoys music and has played guitar since he was a teenager.
